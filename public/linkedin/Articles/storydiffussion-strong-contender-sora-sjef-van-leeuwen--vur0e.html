Comparing StoryDiffusion with Sora

While Sora required a massive 10,000 GPUs for training, StoryDiffusion achieved remarkable results with just eight GPUs.

  • Character Consistency: While Sora has grappled with morphing or inconsistencies in generated videos, StoryDiffusion excels in maintaining consistent characters, including facial features, clothing, and body type.

  • Realism and Adherence to Reality: StoryDiffusion surpasses Sora in its depth of understanding of reality, resulting in videos that feel more natural and cohesive.

  • Scene Cohesion: Unlike Sora, StoryDiffusion seamlessly integrates multiple characters into scenes, overcoming a significant limitation in AI video generation.

  • Compute Power Comparison: What's particularly intriguing is the disparity in compute power between Sora and StoryDiffusion. While Sora required a massive 10,000 GPUs for training, StoryDiffusion achieved remarkable results with just eight GPUs. This stark difference highlights the efficiency and effectiveness of StoryDiffusion's algorithms, paving the way for more accessible and cost-effective AI video generation.
